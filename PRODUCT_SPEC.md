# Product specification

## Product
MYReSolve Executive Risk & Cost Assessment

## Product ID
MR-PRD-001

## Core users
- CEO
- COO
- CFO
- Functional leaders

## Assessment model
Each standard question uses a five-level maturity scale:

1. Critical
2. Developing
3. Established
4. Strong
5. Leading

Each response may also include a confidence rating:

- Low
- Medium
- High

## Core outputs
- Executive maturity score
- Overall risk score
- Department risk scores
- Estimated annual cost of failure
- Top risks
- Recommended 90-day priorities
